Liberal Arts

Liberal Arts encompasses a diverse range of subjects including literature, philosophy, history, and sociology. It emphasizes critical thinking, communication skills, and a broad understanding of human culture and society. Career paths in liberal arts include journalism, education, public policy, and social work.
